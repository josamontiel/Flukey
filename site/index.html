Abstract:

Everyone has a different threat model, if you do not know what a threat model is, a good place to start would be here, but essentially, nearly all threat models require a safe and secure way to generate random passwords. Whether they be in the form of conventional Passwords, PINs or Passphrases, having a way to generate them safely and offline is paramount to getting the most out of your passwords.

The lengths of your PINs, Passwords or Passphrases may vary depending on how protected you wish to remain, this will vary nased on the data that you want to protect. That is where True-Random comes in.
